ABOUT

Designed by Few is a friendly design competition (and party) during the Made by Few conference. Designers face off in real time to benefit a local non-profit while Made by Few attendees watch the design process unfold and have some fun. It's about inspiring the attendees, challenging the designers and getting to know many of the talented creatives that attend the conference from across the world.

ART FEEDS

Each year the Designed by Few competition benefits a local non-profit organization. Over the years, we have selected several organizations that work to inspire through arts education and community engagement.

We are so thrilled that this year’s Designed by Few competition will benefit Art Feeds, a Mobile Arts program dedicated to the growth and healing of children through art.

We aim to assist Art Feeds in their mission by challenging this year’s designers to create a piece that Art Feeds can use in their future work. Project details will be classified until the competition starts.

VISIT

VICTIMS

Each year we look for three of the most talented designers to compete in front of a live audience for the chance to win some sweet prizes and bragging rights. Over the last several months designers from all over the world have applied. We went through each portfolio and narrowed it down to these three amazing designers.

DANIEL

HERRON

Daniel is a Sr. Digital Designer at Keen Footwear in Portland OR. He is a Brand Architect, Lettering Junkie, Amateur Podcaster, and Co-Founder of Bolt Action Apparel. Daniel has a moderately respectable beard. He enjoys Bourbon, claims to enjoy hiking, craft beer, a good Ruben on Rye, and tasteful dry satire.

AMY

JOHNSON

Amy is a Chicago based Visual Interactive Designer for Allstate. Her current focus is mobile interface design and helping develop human interface guidelines. Amy is an active member of the design community and dedicates her free time to mentoring aspiring designers.

KEVIN

CLARK

Kevin is a Design Lead at Shopify where he is responsible for the purchase experience used by thousands of people every day. He’s the organizer of the Montreal Design Club, an event series that brings designers together every few months. He also co-hosts Layout, a podcast about the important topics in the world of design and technology.

LOCATION

This year's Designed by Few competition will be held at the 21c Museum Hotel, located on the Bentonville town square, a short walk from Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art, and The Record.
